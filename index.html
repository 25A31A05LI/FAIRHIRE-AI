How to use FairHire AI

  1. Login: Click 'Login / Sign Up' and enter a company name (or use dummy credentials).
  2. Configuration: In the app, enter your Gemini API Key (or type "DEMO") and specify the job role and required skills.
  3. Upload Resumes: Go to the 'Upload Resumes' tab and select PDF resumes to parse.
  4. Review Candidates: The AI will instantly score and sort candidates blindly based on merit in the 'Detailed Reports' tab.
  5. Analyze Bias: Click 'Detect Bias Analytics' to view your historical hiring fairness using the neon dashboard.
  6. Upload Datasets: You can upload a .csv to the Configuration tab to simulate a company's historical hiring records instantly.
